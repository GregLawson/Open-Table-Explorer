Notes:


Software development can be graphed as movement in Enhancement / Bug Space.

You would think a horizontal line of enhancements without bugs would characterize genius, but more likely it indicates a trivial change (which does not provide any evidence for genius).

The slope of the line is a measure of problem hardness, more than your intelligence.

Also bug-free code is not evidence of genius, as it is often uneconomical to remove low priority bugs.

It is useful to keep bug density low enough that all bugs are independent.

Non-idependent bugs may mask each other.

A proposed fix to one bug may be rejected since it fails a previously passed test by unmasking a dependent bug.
