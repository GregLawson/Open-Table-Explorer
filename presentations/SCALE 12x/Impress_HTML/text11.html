Small commits are less likely to contain more than one bug and provide better granularity for git bisect

Small commits provide possibly greater granularity for upstream cherry picking.

Its difficult to predict what level of granularity upstream will want but consideration include: Only bug fixes into stable production versions

Innocuous changes may include comments, renaming, cruft removal (dead code, meaningless comments), refactoring (code improvement without functional change), adding optional features, or even widespread architectural changes.
